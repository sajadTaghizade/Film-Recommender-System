[PLOT]
"Lucy" is a science fiction action film that follows a young American woman of the same name who is studying in Taipei, Taiwan. She is tricked into working as a drug mule for a Korean mob boss. A bag of a powerful new synthetic drug, CPH4, is surgically implanted into her abdomen. When the bag leaks inside her body, the drug is absorbed into her system. Instead of killing her, it unlocks her brain's potential, allowing her to access more and more of her cerebral capacity. As she evolves, she gains superhuman abilities, including telekinesis, mental time travel, and the ability to control matter. Hunted by the mob, she seeks out a renowned neurologist, Professor Samuel Norman, to help her understand and control her rapidly expanding powers before she transcends human limits entirely.
