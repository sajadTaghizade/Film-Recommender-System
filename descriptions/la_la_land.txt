"La La Land" is a romantic musical comedy-drama that tells the story of Mia, an aspiring actress, and Sebastian, a dedicated jazz musician, who are struggling to make ends meet in modern-day Los Angeles. The film follows their love story as they encourage each other to pursue their dreams, while facing the realities of the competitive entertainment industry. Their relationship is tested as success mounts and they are forced to make decisions that could tear them apart, forcing them to confront the conflict between their love for each other and their career ambitions.
