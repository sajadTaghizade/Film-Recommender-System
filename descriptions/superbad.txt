"Superbad" is a coming-of-age teen comedy that follows two inseparable but co-dependent high school seniors, Seth and Evan. On the verge of graduation and heading to different colleges, they are determined to lose their virginity before they part ways. Their plan hinges on securing alcohol for a massive house party hosted by a girl Seth has a crush on. With the help of their nerdy friend Fogell, who has obtained a ridiculously fake ID under the name "McLovin," the trio embarks on a disastrous and hilarious night-long odyssey filled with encounters with eccentric police officers, angry partygoers, and their own separation anxiety.
