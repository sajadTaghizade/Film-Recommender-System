Based on the novel by Stephenie Meyer, "Twilight" is a romantic fantasy film. It follows seventeen-year-old Bella Swan, who moves to the small, perpetually rainy town of Forks, Washington, to live with her father. There, she becomes intrigued by the mysterious and alluring Edward Cullen. She soon discovers that Edward and his family are vampires who have chosen to abstain from drinking human blood. A dangerous and unconventional romance blossoms between the mortal Bella and the immortal Edward, which is complicated by the arrival of a coven of malevolent, nomadic vampires who threaten Bella's life.
