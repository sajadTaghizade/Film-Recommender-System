"The Big Lebowski" is a cult classic crime comedy about Jeffrey "The Dude" Lebowski, a slacker and avid bowler in Los Angeles. His laid-back life is thrown into chaos when he is mistaken for a millionaire of the same name. After two thugs urinate on his rug to coerce him into paying a debt he knows nothing about, The Dude seeks recompense from his wealthy namesake. This entanglement leads him and his bowling buddies, the volatile Walter Sobchak and the meek Donny Kerabatsos, into a complex world of kidnapping, nihilists, pornographers, and millionaires, all while The Dude just wants his rug back.
