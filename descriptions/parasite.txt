[PLOT]
"Parasite" is a South Korean black comedy thriller that follows the Kim family—father Ki-taek, mother Chung-sook, son Ki-woo, and daughter Ki-jung—who live in a squalid semi-basement apartment. They are all unemployed and struggle to make a living. Through a stroke of luck, Ki-woo gets a job as an English tutor for the daughter of the wealthy Park family. One by one, the Kims cunningly infiltrate the Park household, posing as unrelated, highly qualified workers. However, their new parasitic life is threatened when they discover a dark secret hidden within the luxurious home, leading to a violent and tragic confrontation between the two families.
