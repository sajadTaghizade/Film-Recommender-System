"Liar Liar" is a fantasy comedy starring Jim Carrey as Fletcher Reede, a fast-talking, career-driven lawyer and pathological liar. His constant lying has made him successful in his profession but has destroyed his relationship with his ex-wife, Audrey, and his young son, Max. Disappointed by his father's broken promises, Max makes a birthday wish that for just one day, his dad cannot tell a lie. The wish miraculously comes true, and Fletcher finds himself physically incapable of uttering any falsehoods. This curse becomes a major problem as he has to navigate a career-defining court case, forcing him to confront the consequences of his dishonesty and become a better father.
