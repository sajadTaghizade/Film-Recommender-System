"The Conjuring" is a supernatural horror film based on the real-life case files of paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren. In 1971, Carolyn and Roger Perron move into a secluded old farmhouse in Rhode Island with their five daughters. They soon discover that the house is plagued by a sinister and increasingly terrifying supernatural presence. Desperate and frightened, they contact the Warrens for help. The Warrens investigate and find that the house is haunted by a malevolent demonic entity tied to a dark and tragic history. They must use all their skills and spiritual strength to confront the powerful entity and save the family from the escalating horror before it consumes them all.
