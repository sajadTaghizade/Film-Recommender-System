"Avengers: Endgame" is the culmination of 22 interconnected films in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Following the devastating events of "Avengers: Infinity War," where the villainous Thanos wiped out half of all life in the universe with the Infinity Stones, the remaining Avengers are left scattered and defeated. Five years later, a glimmer of hope emerges when Ant-Man returns from the Quantum Realm. The surviving heroes, including Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Black Widow, and Hulk, devise a desperate plan to travel back in time to retrieve the Infinity Stones before Thanos can. Their mission is a perilous "time heist" that forces them to revisit key moments in their past and make ultimate sacrifices to restore balance to the universe.
