[PLOT]
"Groundhog Day" is a fantasy comedy about Phil Connors, a cynical and arrogant television meteorologist. He is sent to the small town of Punxsutawney, Pennsylvania, to cover the annual Groundhog Day ceremony, an assignment he despises. After a blizzard traps him in the town, he wakes up the next morning to find that it is Groundhog Day all over again. He is caught in a time loop, forced to relive the same day repeatedly. Initially, he uses this for his own hedonistic purposes, but despair and loneliness soon set in. Eventually, he begins to re-examine his life and priorities, using the endless time to better himself and win the heart of his producer, Rita Hanson.
