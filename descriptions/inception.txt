[PLOT]
"Inception" is a science fiction action film about Dom Cobb, a highly skilled thief who specializes in "extraction": stealing valuable information from deep within the subconscious during the dream state when the mind is at its most vulnerable. Cobb's rare ability has made him a coveted player in the world of corporate espionage but has also cost him everything he has ever loved. He is offered a chance at redemption—a seemingly impossible task called "inception," which involves planting an idea into a target's subconscious rather than stealing one. If he succeeds, it could be the perfect crime, but a dangerous enemy anticipates his every move.
