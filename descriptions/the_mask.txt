"The Mask" is a superhero comedy film based on the Dark Horse comic book series. It tells the story of Stanley Ipkiss, a kind-hearted but socially awkward and timid bank clerk. His life changes dramatically when he finds an ancient wooden mask by a river. When he puts on the mask, he is transformed into "The Mask," a green-faced, zoot-suited, cartoonish trickster with superhuman abilities and a complete lack of inhibition. As The Mask, Stanley can do anything his heart desires, which he uses to exact revenge on his tormentors and woo a beautiful nightclub singer named Tina Carlyle. However, he also runs afoul of a local mob boss, Dorian Tyrell, who wants the mask's power for himself.
